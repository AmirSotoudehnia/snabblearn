Quick Help

  • Remove Ads / Restore Purchases: In the app open Settings → Restore Purchases. If it doesn’t restore, reinstall the app and try again, then email us.
  • Notifications: iOS Settings → Notifications → SnabbLearn → Allow notifications. Inside the app, enable daily reminders.
  • Ads in testing: Test builds may show reservation/test ads; production behavior can differ.
